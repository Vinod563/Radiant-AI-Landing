## Project Overview

**Client:** StellarWorks (global creative studio)
**Category:** Branding & Marketing, Brand Strategy
**Duration:** 4 weeks

Despite delivering quality work, StellarWorks' visual identity failed to communicate their expertise and innovation level to prospective clients.

---

## Challenges

- Brand visuals felt outdated and generic
- No competitive differentiation in the marketplace
- Unclear messaging and market positioning
- Inconsistent application of design elements across touchpoints

---

## Solution (4-Part Strategy)

1. **Brand Strategy & Positioning** – Clarified the studio's vision and market role
2. **Visual Identity Design** – Bold logo system, color framework, typeface selection
3. **Design System & Guidelines** – Consistent layouts and visual rules
4. **Marketing Structure** – Scalable systems for future expansion
