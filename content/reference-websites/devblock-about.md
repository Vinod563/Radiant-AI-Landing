## Company Overview
DevBlock is a technology consulting firm of "thinkers, innovators, problem solvers, and creators" emphasizing collaborative design thinking with clients and stakeholders.

## Mission
"Driven by purpose and results." Creating products that emphasize human interactions, needs, thoughts, and emotions while building visual identity systems reflecting brand values.

## Core Values
1. **Clear Guidance** — Creating realistic, accurate, simple processes
2. **Quality of Work** — Prioritizing deliverable excellence
3. **Accountability** — Managing expectations responsibly
4. **Transparency** — Using clear communication and agile planning
5. **Mutual Success** — Positioning clients as heroes
