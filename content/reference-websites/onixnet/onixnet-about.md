## Content Writing Patterns (About Page)

### How They Tell Their Story
- **Partnership-led narrative:** Company story told through the lens of partnerships and client success
- **Credential-forward:** Awards and certifications featured prominently (16X Partner of the Year)
- **Scale signals:** Employee count (500+ agents), Fortune 500 client base
- **Longevity:** "20+ Years of Excellence" establishes trust through tenure

### Messaging Tone
- Confident but not boastful
- Achievement-oriented — every claim backed by a credential
- Partnership language: "partner with," "collaborate," "together"
- Transformation-focused: about enabling client outcomes, not self-celebration

### Content Structure Pattern
1. Mission/Vision statement (answer-first, Minto style)
2. Key metrics bar (3-4 hero stats)
3. Partnership story (how they got here)
4. Awards/recognition section
5. Team/culture section
6. CTA (join us / work with us)

### Key Takeaway for Radiant
- About pages should lead with impact metrics, not founding stories
- Use the "partnership journey" narrative — not "we were founded in..."
- Certifications and awards should be visual, not text-heavy
- End with a dual CTA: one for clients, one for talent
