# BCG Insights: Agentic AI Opportunity for Tech Service Providers
**Source:** BCG Research — "The Three Net-New Value Pools"
**Date Added:** 2026-03-10
**Purpose:** Strategic content framework for positioning Radiant Digital in the agentic AI market

## The Three Net-New Value Pools

BCG identifies three areas where new demand more than offsets efficiency-driven contraction:

### 1. Build-Deploy-Run for Agentic Solutions
- Use case design and agent workflow engineering
- Integration with core enterprise systems (ERP, CRM)
- Embedding agents into customer and employee journeys
- **Radiant Alignment:** Product Development & Integration + AI Practice

### 2. Expansion of Addressable Scope
- Agents overcome language and domain constraints
- New categories of work become outsourceable
- Data-powered services that weren't previously viable
- **Radiant Alignment:** Analytics, Data Science & AI + Skilled Workforce Solutions

### 3. Oversight and Governance
- Continuous AI-for-operations support
- Drift detection and human-in-the-loop escalation
- Audit trails and compliance reporting
- Model risk management (especially regulated industries)
- **Radiant Alignment:** Cloud Transformation + Digital Strategy & Experience

## The Four Expectation Gaps

These gaps are content GOLDMINES — each is a section or page opportunity:

### Gap 1: Priority Misalignment
- **Problem:** Providers underserve demand in IT operations, cybersecurity, application implementation
- **Content Angle:** "We focus where demand is highest — IT ops, cybersecurity, and app implementation"
- **Radiant Opportunity:** Highlight these as core strengths, not afterthoughts

### Gap 2: Efficiency Gap
- **Problem:** Enterprises expect 30–40% productivity improvements; providers commit to only 6–15%
- **Content Angle:** Lead with bold efficiency claims backed by case study data
- **Radiant Opportunity:** If Radiant can commit to 25-40% ranges with proof, this is a massive differentiator
- **Metrics to Target:**
  - 30-40% productivity improvement (match enterprise expectations)
  - Show specific case studies hitting these numbers

### Gap 3: Proof Gap
- **Problem:** Nearly 60% of enterprises report no measurable TCO improvement in agentic AI deals
- **Content Angle:** "Every engagement comes with measurable TCO benchmarks"
- **Radiant Opportunity:** Outcome-based pricing, TCO dashboards, pre/post metrics in every case study
- **Key Stat for Content:** "60% of enterprises see no TCO improvement — we guarantee ours do"

### Gap 4: Commercial Model Gap
- **Problem:** 70%+ of buyers prefer outcome-linked contracts; 60% of providers still use T&M or fixed-price
- **Content Angle:** "Outcome-linked engagement models that align our success with yours"
- **Radiant Opportunity:** Promote outcome-based, gain-sharing, or performance-linked contracts
- **Key Stat for Content:** "70% of enterprises want outcome-linked deals — that's how we work"

## BCG's 5 Winning Imperatives (Mapped to Radiant)

### 1. Reimagine the Portfolio
- Embed agentic capabilities across existing offerings
- Build new services anchored to outcome-centric propositions
- **Radiant Content:** Position AI Fabric as the connective layer across all 6 service areas

### 2. Sell Proven Impact
- Validated ROI and engineered POCs are prerequisites for winning deals
- **Radiant Content:** Lead every service page with quantified results; make POC offerings prominent

### 3. Orchestrate the Partner Ecosystem
- 90%+ of providers deliver agentic engagements through partners
- Competitive advantage = how well partnerships are orchestrated
- **Radiant Content:** Showcase technology partnerships (AWS, Azure, GCP, etc.) and integration depth

### 4. Transform Delivery Operations
- Expect 10–20% shrinkage in delivery pyramid over 24 months
- Humans shift from executing tasks to orchestrating agents
- **Radiant Content:** Position Radiant as already operating with AI-augmented delivery teams

### 5. Reshape Talent
- Traditional roles slowing; AI-native roles growing at 40–50% CAGR
- AI product engineers, chatbot developers = fastest-growing roles
- **Radiant Content:** Highlight AI-native talent in Skilled Workforce Solutions; emphasize reskilling capabilities
