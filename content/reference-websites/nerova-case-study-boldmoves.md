## Client Overview

**Company:** BoldMoves.ai (SaaS platform, competitive market)
**Challenge:** Strong product-market fit but weak organic visibility and heavy reliance on expensive paid acquisition channels.

---

## Problems Identified

- Low keyword rankings and technical SEO issues
- Unclear messaging reducing conversions and trust
- Competitors dominating search results
- High dependency on costly paid channels, limiting scalability

---

## Solution (4-Phase Approach)

1. **Product Discovery** – User behavior analysis and competitive benchmarking to identify optimization opportunities
2. **Brand Alignment** – Reorganized site structure into clearer conversion funnel: hero clarity → product value → proof → CTA
3. **UX Flow Optimization** – Premium design language with refined typography, spacing, elevated color palette
4. **Scalable UI System** – Modular components with strong visual hierarchy and interactive guidance elements
