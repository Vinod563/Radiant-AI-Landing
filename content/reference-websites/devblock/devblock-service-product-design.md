**Source:** https://devblock.net/services/product-design
**Fetched:** 2026-03-06

---

## Overview
Takes ideas from concept to pixel-perfect design, ensuring every user experience is visually stunning and functionally seamless.

## Core Service Areas

### Research & Strategy
- **User Interviews** — Customer needs and behavior insights
- **Usability Testing** — Resolving design issues before launch
- **Heuristic Evaluation** — Catching usability problems early
- **Competitive Analysis** — Market position evaluation

### Design Development
- **Information Architecture** — Navigational structure for UX
- **Wireframing** — Visual blueprints focused on core elements
- **UI + Visual Design** — High-fidelity balancing aesthetics and function
- **Branding** — Distinctive identity and customer loyalty
- **Design Systems** — Scalable, future-proof design frameworks

### Validation & Implementation
- **Prototyping + User Testing** — Interactive prototypes for iterative refinement
- **Implementation + Launch** — Documented designs via Figma for smooth dev handoff

## Process Phases
Product Discovery → Strategy → Problem Definition → Content Strategy → Requirements Gathering → Research → UX/UI Design → Wireframing → Information Architecture → Personas → Prototypes → Branding → Design Systems → Analytics Review

## Key Differentiators
- Data-driven design approach
- Figma-based collaboration
- Seamless development handoff to minimize rework
