## Overview

**Main Hub for Large-Scale Transformation**

Radiant Digital positions the Americas as "the proving grounds for the most complex and large-scale environments." The region comprises federal, state, and local governments, commercial enterprises, and major educational institutions operating at significant scale. The company is headquartered in the United States and serves customers across the U.S., Canada, and the British Virgin Islands.

## Core Philosophy

Organizations throughout this region expect transformation initiatives to deliver:
- Continuity and confidence in operations
- Measurable progress and results
- AI-first approaches focused on operational resilience
- Innovation within regulatory constraints

## Service Offerings: AI Fabric

Radiant Digital integrates artificial intelligence across six primary enablers:

1. **Digital Strategy and Experience** - Accelerate experience design with AI-driven insights
2. **Product Development and Integration** - Enhance delivery speed through AI-enabled development
3. **Cloud Transformation** - Optimize infrastructure for improved scalability
4. **Analytics, Data and AI** - Convert data into real-time intelligence for decision-making
5. **Organizational Transformation** - Empower teams through AI enablement
6. **Skilled Workforce Solutions** - Deploy AI-assisted talent sourcing
